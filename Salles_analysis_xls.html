Kemp Sales Performance and Return Behavior Analysis

Scenario Overview:

Kemp is a luxury retail company operating across multiple countries, offering a diverse range of product categories including Apparel, Books, Electronics, Groceries, and Home Decor.

Recently, company leadership has observed a noticeable increase in product returns and is growing concerned that delivery delays may be impacting customer satisfaction and driving return behavior.

I was contacted as a data analyst to investigate these trends. My role involves analyzing both sales performance and product return patterns, with a particular focus on determining whether delivery time influences the likelihood of returns.
